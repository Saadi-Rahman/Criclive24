All-time Bangladesh World Cup XI

Bangladesh have had several standout individual performers in World Cup matches, starting with Minhajul Abedin and Khaled Mahmud, who won Player-of-the-Match awards in the 1999 tournament, against Scotland and Pakistan respectively. Six others have also been Player of the Match - Mohammad Ashraful and Imrul Kayes twice each.

Mohammad Rafique, Shakib Al Hasan and Rubel Hossain have performed strongly in crucial games. Mashrafe Mortaza nips Habibul Bashar as captain in my XI, having taken the team to the knockouts of the tournament for the first time, in 2015.
